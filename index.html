OUR MISSION

CRICKET FOR A Cause

Access to Cricket Equipment

We believe every young player deserves quality equipment. Your support helps us provide bats, balls, pads, and gear to kids who otherwise couldn't afford them.

Growing the Next Generation

Cricket is booming in Pittsburgh. We're here to nurture young talent, build a community around the sport, and ensure the next generation has every opportunity to play.

Community-Driven Impact

Run entirely by high school students, the Pitt Charity Cup proves that young people can make a real difference. Every dollar raised goes directly to supporting youth cricket.
