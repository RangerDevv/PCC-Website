RULES Summary

01
7 Overs Per Innings
Each team bowls and bats for a maximum of 7 overs per innings to keep games fast and exciting.
02
8 Players Per Team
Teams consist of 8 players. All players must be under 18 years of age at the time of the tournament.
03
Tennis Ball Only
All matches are played with a tennis ball (taped or untaped as decided pre-match) for safety.
04
No LBW
LBW dismissals are not applicable. This simplifies umpiring and keeps the game beginner-friendly.
05
Retirement at 25 Runs
A batsman must retire upon reaching 25 runs to ensure fair play and give all players a chance to bat.
06
Max 2 Overs Per Bowler
No bowler may bowl more than 2 overs in a single innings to encourage team participation.
07
One Bounce Catches
One-bounce catches are allowed for dismissals, making fielding more accessible for all skill levels.
08
Super Over for Ties
In the event of a tie, a Super Over will be played to determine the winner — 1 over per side, 2 batsmen each.
